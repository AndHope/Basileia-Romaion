###
### This file is used to modify cultures from vanilla using the INJECT/REPLACE functionality instead of overridding the whole vanilla file. (New BR cultures are defined in the br_new_culture.txt file).
### If you want to add something to a vanilla culture just use INJECT on the culture, but if you want to replace a single the feature (like an obsession) of a culture you will have to REPLACE the whole culture.
### If you want remove a culture just do this:
### INJECT:<culture>= {
### 	heritage = heritage_removed_culture #BR
### 	language = language_removed_culture #BR
###	}
###
