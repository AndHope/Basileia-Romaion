[h3]Features:[/h3]
- New alternative history scenario
- Mod is architextured to load and run as fast as possible should be much faster than the base game map.
- New nations: Basileía Romaíon, Holy Roman Empire, Hungary, Galicia, Al-Andalus, Asturias, Aragon, Navara, New Granada, Atlantide, Armenia, Arevelyan, Georgia, Wallachia, Moldova, Serbia, Bosnia, Montenegro, North Africa, Aegypt, Abyssinia, Venice and Genoa, Abbasid Caliphate, Nubia, Mali Empire, Zanzibar, Adal Sultanate, Elysium, Vinland, Aztec, Inca, Mesopotamia, Assyria, Pommeria, United Baltic Duchies, Polish Lithuanian Commonwealth, Pskov, Novgorod, Perm, Muscovy, Smolensk, Ryazan, Ruthenia, Golden Horde, Khazer Khanate, Mughal Empire, Franconia, Swabia & dozens of new decentralized nations in Amercia and Africa.
- New journal entries, decisions, events.
- New Cultures: Romaíoi, Boulgaroí, Assyrian, Syriac, Coptic, Albanoí, Varangian, Andalusian, Granadian, Elysian, Vinlander, Nubian & Vlach.
- New Religions: Apostolic, Krstjani, Nestorian, Iconoclast, Bogomilist, Hellenic, Norse, Zoroastrian, Totemism, Nahuatl, Inti, Tengri and Mesoamerican.
- Unique Techs, Modifiers & Traits.
- New Monuments: Hippodrome, Great Palace, Senate, Statue of Elysium & More.
- New Subject types: Exarchate & Themata.
- New Government types: Merchant Republic, Exarchate, Themata, Katepaníkion, Khanate & Caliphate.
- New Ruler Titles: Basileus/Basilissa, Exarchos, Strategos, Katepanō, Doge, Satrap, Khan & Caliph.
- Anatolia, Greece and Balkans use their medieval Greek names
- America to use Roman, Native & Viking names depending on owner.
- Anatolia uses European city graphics instead of Arabic.
- Golden Horde provinces use asian graphics instead of Arabic and or European.
- Lots more
